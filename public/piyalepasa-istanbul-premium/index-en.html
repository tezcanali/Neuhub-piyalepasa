ABOUT THE PROJECT

A Privileged
Living Space

Piyalepaşa Istanbul Premium, located right next to Piyalepaşa Istanbul, the largest urban transformation project realized by the private sector in Turkey, offers you a life full of privileges.

It creates a spacious environment where you can live with its terrace and garden options, high ceilings and large windows, as well as balconies specially designed for each flat.. Indoor parking and special storage areas make your life easier..

In Piyalepaşa Istanbul Premium, where your living spaces are not limited to your apartment, you will be able to expand your life as you wish with its unique lobby area, entertainment and activity areas.

Moreover, Polat Piyalepaşa Bazaar is only a step away with its playgrounds for children, leading galleries of art, rich gastronomic culture, where you can shop in the open air!

How to Go?

Coming via TEM; If you are driving in the direction of the bridge, after reaching Okmeydanı Junction via Hasdal, Okmeydanı connection road, continue in the direction of Kasımpaşa and you are on Piyalepaşa Boulevard. After approximately 1 km, you will see Piyalepaşa Istanbul Premium on your left.

Coming via E5; If you are driving in the direction of the bridge, after reaching Okmeydanı Junction via Hasdal, Okmeydanı connection road, continue in the direction of Kasımpaşa and you are on Piyalepeşa Boulevard. After approximately 1 km, you will see Piyalepaşa Istanbul Premium on your left.
